Coron Palawan

Coron is a tropical island in the province of Palawan in the Philippines. It is the third-largest island in the Calamian Islands in northern Palawan. The island is known for several Japanese shipwrecks of World War II vintage, which make it a popular destination for world-class wreck diving. In addition to wreck diving, the island offers limestone karst landscapes, beautiful beaches, crystal-clear freshwater lakes, and shallow-water coral reefs. The entire area is protected by several legal proclamations due to its unique ecological features.
